ESSArch - A Digital Archival Solution

ESSArch is an open source archival solution compliant to the OAIS ISO-standard, developed together with the National Archives of Sweden and Norway.

ESSArch consist of software components that provide functionality for Pre-Ingest, Ingest, Preservation, Access, Data Management, Administration and Management.

Every software component of ESSArch can be used individually and also be easily integrated together to provide overall functionality for producers, archivists and consumers.
